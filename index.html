About Microtel Inn and Suites

Microtel Inn and Suites by Wyndham is a three-story, 53-room, interior-corridor hotel with a mixture of standard guestrooms and suites. The hotel will have contemporary finishes, attractive to today’s oil and gas business traveler. Every bed is queen size and each room is equipped with a work desk, armoire, large flat panel television with HD channels, wired and wireless Internet, alarm clock-radio, and phones with voicemail. Suites have longer bays that provide for a sitting area and a wet bar area equipped with a microwave oven, small refrigerator, and wet bar sink.

Microtel Inn and Suites is part of the Wyndham Hotel Group, a leading lodging company with approximately 7,210 hotels with 607,000 guest rooms in the United States and 64 other countries. It manages one of the world’s largest hotel loyalty programs, Wyndham Rewards, with over 7.0 million members.
